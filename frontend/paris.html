About the Trip

Experience the timeless charm of Paris, where romance, history, and art blend seamlessly. From iconic landmarks to cozy cafés, this journey introduces you to the soul of France.

Walk beneath the Eiffel Tower, explore world-renowned museums, cruise along the Seine River, and wander through charming streets filled with art, fashion, and history.

Designed for explorers aged 18–35, this trip balances sightseeing, culture, food, and unforgettable memories.
