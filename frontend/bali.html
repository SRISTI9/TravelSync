About the Trip

Embark on a thrilling adventure through Bali and the breathtaking Gili Islands, where tropical landscapes, vibrant culture, and unforgettable experiences come together. This journey takes you from the lively beaches of Kuta to the serene rice terraces of Ubud, the dramatic cliffs of Nusa Penida, and the laid-back island vibes of Gili Trawangan.

You’ll witness the iconic Kecak and Fire Dance at Uluwatu Temple, explore the lush Tegallalang Rice Terraces, and marvel at stunning waterfalls hidden within Bali’s jungles. Indulge in unique local experiences, from tasting the world-famous Kopi Luwak to cycling through picturesque villages.

As the journey continues, crystal-clear waters, beach sunsets, snorkeling adventures, and vibrant nightlife await you on the Gili Islands. Designed for young explorers aged 18–35, this trip perfectly balances adventure, relaxation, and social connections.

Whether you’re traveling solo or with friends, expect unforgettable memories, lifelong friendships, and stories you’ll cherish forever.

FAQs

How many travelers can join the adventure on this Bali trip? +
We keep it tight, usually rolling with a crew of 18–20 awesome individuals. Smaller groups mean better bonding and exclusive experiences.
What kind of food can we expect during the trip? +
Daily breakfasts are included. You’ll get to try local Balinese cuisine, international options, and plenty of café hopping.
What’s the physical hustle level on this trip? +
Moderate. Activities like surfing, hikes, and cycling are included, but everything is beginner-friendly.
What luggage works best for Bali? +
A backpack or medium-sized trolley works best. Bali involves ferries, bikes, and walking.
Can I rely on cards or should I carry cash? +
Cards work in most places, but carrying some cash is recommended for local markets and small cafés.
What are the accommodations like? +
Comfortable 3/4-star hotels in prime locations on a twin-sharing basis.
Can I request a roommate? +
Absolutely! If you’re traveling solo, we’ll match you with a same-gender traveler.
Is travel insurance mandatory? +
Travel insurance is highly recommended for medical and baggage safety.
Are flights included in the package? +
No, international flights are not included. You’re free to book from any city.
What happens if I need to cancel? +
Cancellation policies depend on how close the departure date is. Full details will be shared at booking.
